Hey everyone! Let's chat about globalization and migration while exploring conditionals in English. These topics are all around us, so it's a great way to practice.
Hola a tothom! Parlem sobre la globalització i la migració mentre explorem els condicionals en anglès. Aquests temes són a tot arreu, així que és una gran manera de practicar.
First up is the zero conditional. We use it for facts or things that are always true. For example: "If people migrate, they adapt to new cultures." This means whenever people migrate, this happens.
Primer tenim el condicional zero. L'utilitzem per a fets o coses que sempre són veritat. Per exemple: "If people migrate, they adapt to new cultures." Això significa que sempre que la gent migra, això passa.
In a negative way: "If communities don't embrace diversity, tensions arise." So if something doesn't happen, another thing occurs.
En forma negativa: "If communities don't embrace diversity, tensions arise." Així que si alguna cosa no passa, una altra cosa ocorre.
As a question: "What happens if people move to a new country?" We're asking about the usual result of this action.
Com a pregunta: "What happens if people move to a new country?" Estem preguntant sobre el resultat habitual d'aquesta acció.
Next is the first conditional, used for real possibilities in the future. For instance: "If I learn Spanish, I'll communicate better when I move to Spain." This is a real chance in the future.
A continuació, el primer condicional, que s'utilitza per a possibilitats reals en el futur. Per exemple: "If I learn Spanish, I'll communicate better when I move to Spain." Aquesta és una possibilitat real en el futur.
Negatively: "If we don't address climate change, more people will be forced to migrate." Here, not doing something leads to a future consequence.
En forma negativa: "If we don't address climate change, more people will be forced to migrate." Aquí, no fer alguna cosa porta a una conseqüència futura.
Question form: "Will you migrate if you get that job offer abroad?" Asking about a possible future action.
Forma de pregunta: "Will you migrate if you get that job offer abroad?" Preguntant sobre una possible acció futura.
Then there's the second conditional, for unreal or hypothetical situations now or in the future. Example: "If I had a visa, I would move to Canada." I don't have a visa, but I'm imagining.
Després hi ha el segon condicional, per a situacions irreals o hipotètiques ara o en el futur. Exemple: "If I had a visa, I would move to Canada." No tinc un visat, però estic imaginant.
Negative form: "If globalization didn't exist, we wouldn't enjoy international cuisines." Imagining a world that's different from ours.
Forma negativa: "If globalization didn't exist, we wouldn't enjoy international cuisines." Imaginant un món diferent del nostre.
Question: "What would you do if you lived in another country?" Asking about a hypothetical situation.
Pregunta: "What would you do if you lived in another country?" Preguntant sobre una situació hipotètica.
Now, the third conditional talks about unreal situations in the past. For example: "If she had studied international law, she would have worked at the UN." She didn't study it, so it didn't happen.
Ara, el tercer condicional parla de situacions irreals en el passat. Per exemple: "If she had studied international law, she would have worked at the UN." Ella no va estudiar-ho, així que no va passar.
Negative way: "If they hadn't migrated, they wouldn't have experienced new cultures." They did migrate, so they did experience them.
En forma negativa: "If they hadn't migrated, they wouldn't have experienced new cultures." Van migrar, així que van experimentar-les.
Question form: "What would have happened if you hadn't moved abroad?" Wondering about a past that didn't occur.
Forma de pregunta: "What would have happened if you hadn't moved abroad?" Preguntant sobre un passat que no va ocórrer.
Lastly, the mixed conditional combines past and present. For instance: "If I had saved money, I would be traveling now." My past action affects my present situation.
Finalment, el condicional mixt combina passat i present. Per exemple: "If I had saved money, I would be traveling now." La meva acció passada afecta la meva situació present.
Negative example: "If he hadn't left his country, he wouldn't be bilingual now." A past event influencing the present.
Exemple negatiu: "If he hadn't left his country, he wouldn't be bilingual now." Un esdeveniment passat que influeix en el present.
Question: "If you had accepted that job overseas, where would you be living now?" Mixing past decisions with present outcomes.
Pregunta: "If you had accepted that job overseas, where would you be living now?" Barrejant decisions passades amb resultats presents.
So, conditionals help us talk about possibilities, imaginations, and consequences related to globalization and migration. Pretty handy, right? Let me know if you have any questions!
Així doncs, els condicionals ens ajuden a parlar de possibilitats, imaginacions i conseqüències relacionades amb la globalització i la migració. Força útils, oi? Feu-me saber si teniu alguna pregunta!
